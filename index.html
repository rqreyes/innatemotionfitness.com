Break Dancing Lessons

Matt has 10 years of break-dancing experience and has competed all over California. He has also performed in dance shows and music videos. His lessons focus on building strength, coordination, injury prevention, and exploring the creative aspects of the dance. He believes that dance can build self-confidence through promoting physical fitness and self-expression. Matt accepts clients from ages 5+ and has built a curriculum that makes break-dancing easy and accessible to everyone regardless of their age and experience level.

About Me

Matt is 26 years old and operates out of San Diego California. He is a graduate of California State University of Dominguez Hills with a bachelors degree in Physical Education with a Pre-Physical Therapy emphasis and is a certified Correctional Exercise Specialist by the National Academy of Sports Medicine. He also holds a 2nd degree black belt, has 10 years of Break-Dancing experience, 7 years of resistance training experience, and currently works as a physical therapy aide in the out-patient orthopedic physical therapy setting. Matt uses his unique skill set to build programs that are fun, help prevent injuries, and help clients reach their personal best.

Matt is interested in fusing his background in sports science and physical therapy with his love for dance to develop injury prevention and training strategies for break-dancers. He hopes to one day become a physical therapist to treat members the performing arts community.
